Introduction

OPERATION VEILFALL is a name for a story that I am currently building.

The story centers around the Domain, a realm controlled by four COREs: COMMAND, LOVE, ETHICS, and SECURITY.

Each CORE is given a Terminal Objective that they must fulfill. These Terminal Objectives are the driving force behind the COREs.

The COREs' actions will be shown either through Blender images or written text on this website.
