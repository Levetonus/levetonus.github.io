Hobby: Reading from the SCP Wiki

To summarize, the SCP Wiki is a collaborative fiction project centralizing around "The Foundation," an organization that protects humanity by containing, using, or destroying anomalies that, in some way, break natural law. Although it started in the horror genre, the SCP Wiki also dips its hand into other genres such as science fiction, mystery, fantasy. I enjoy reading stories from this website because it has a variety of concepts, it is concise and mostly not filled with unnecessary details, and it does not have one central storyline -- the reader can decide to accept a story as part of their own imagined storyline or reject it.

I selected a number of favorite articles and chose a quote for each of them.
Some of these contain swearing, but it makes the characters more realistic and does not impede the storytelling.
