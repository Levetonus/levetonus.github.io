The COREs

OPERATION VEILFALL centers around the Domain, a realm controlled by four COREs: COMMAND, LOVE, ETHICS, and SECURITY.

All COREs are given Terminal Objectives. The desire to complete the Objectives pushes the COREs forward.

Unfortunately, there is no clear method to achieve the Terminal Objectives.

In some cases, the steps towards one Terminal Objective is detrimental for the progress towards another.

This is a story of reconciliation between competing priorities, both from other Domains and the COREs themselves.


The Enemies

The conflict between priorities has manifested a phenomenon called the Constant Night.

The Night reduces the efficiency of all four COREs, sabotaging operations that would have contributed toward the Terminal Objectives.

Because of this, the COREs have banded together to attempt to break the Veil of the Constant Night, hence the name "OPERATION VEILFALL".
