The COREs

OPERATION VEILFALL is a story centered around the Domain, a realm controlled by four COREs: COMMAND, LOVE, ETHICS, and SECURITY.

All COREs are given Terminal Objectives. The desire to complete the Objectives pushes the COREs forward.

Unfortunately, the path towards completion is foggy, unclear, and uncertain.

In some cases, the steps toward one Terminal Objective are detrimental toward another Terminal Objective.

This is a story of reconciliation between competing priorities, both from other Domains and the COREs themselves.

The Enemies

The conflict between priorities has manifested a phenomenon called the Constant Night.

The Night reduces the efficiency of all four COREs, sabotaging operations that would have contributed toward the Terminal Objectives.

Because of this, the COREs have banded together to attempt to break the veil of the Constant Night.

An associated enemy is called the Annihilating Darkness, which appears when the Night is particularly overwhelming.
