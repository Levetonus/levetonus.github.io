The Timeline System

OPERATION VEILFALL is split into two timelines: the Main Timeline, and the Experimental Timeline.

The Experimental Timeline is, in universe, a simulation controlled by the Main Timeline.

Out of universe, the Experimental Timeline is where I build stuff that have no basis in my actual, personal life.

Most contributions to the Experimental Timeline are inspired by songs that I listen to.

The Main Timeline, on the other hand, is inspired by my actual emotions and what has actually happened to me.
